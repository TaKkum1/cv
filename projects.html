Projects


  • The Automatic Irrigation System           March 2020 - May 2020

    • Designed an irrigation system automatically watering, temperature controlling, changing pH value, illuminating for a spider plant,
      which are fully controlled by Arduino through humidity sensor, temperature sensor, pH sensor, and the sunlight intensity sensor.
    • Tested the code of the pH sensor in C/C++ on Arduino Uno. Used scripting language to test what substances can increase or decrease pH quickly
      and do less damage to the plant and spider plant.
    • Adding baking soda when pH is lower than 6.0, and vinegar will be added when pH is above 6.5. And when the pushbutton was pressed,
      the OLED display will show the pH value for 3 seconds.

  • The Battle-Bot

    • Designed an 8x6x5 inches rear drive battle-bot with a hammer by using the System Development Life Cycle. The hammer is installed at
      top of the battle-bot and controlled by another motor.
    • Analyzed whether hammer or a saw is better for a combat. Programmed the code of controlling the hammer using C on Arduino and the
      power of hammer by changing the speed of motor and height of hammer. And debugged battle-bot when controller was unable to control the battle-bot.
    • Coordinating the balance of speed of motor and height of hammer to maximum the hammer damage. Battle-bot have a more stable connection
      with controller by reinforcing. Reproduce and enhance the battle-bot by repeating test.

  • Carrying Boe-Bot

    • Built a 6x4x4 inches carrying Boe-Bot that can clamp an empty can and move when it capture high decibel sounds.
    • Tested the minimum distance that the Boe-Bot need to take a turn when receive the Ultrasonic sensor signal. Also, I tested what sensors
      are ideal for detecting the target object through different factors, including distance between Boe-Bot and empty can, can temperature, signal angle, and object size.
    • Making a 90 degrees right turn when the ultrasonic sensor receive the signal after detected anything is within 10 of the bot on the horizontal.
      So that the Boe-Bot can ensure its own safety when transporting objects by capturing sounds through the sound sensor.
